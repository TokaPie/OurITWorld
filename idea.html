E-Commerce

Topic

The topic that our group has chosen to work on is E-Commerce. This is one of the most popular shopping platforms that are widely used all over the world and really exploded in popularity during the pandemic. E-Commerce experienced a spike in both popularity and users because, during the Covid-19 lockdown, everyone had to stay at their homes mandatory which made them can’t go out and do shopping directly, so people looked for alternative ways to do so. Not only has this behavior stayed with people past the epidemic, but it has also fundamentally changed the way people approach buying products: extremely convenient, fast and the customer can contact the seller right away if they need more information. It also has changed the way businesses approach the public market and their clients, providing more methods for small businesses to sell their products without going through existing stores and deal with the high expenses associated with opening them in major cities such as Ho Chi Minh or Hanoi without needing much capital funding.

The advantages and benefits of E-Commerce are proven through the popularity of major platforms such as Shopee or Lazada in Vietnam, which has become the preferred way to do your shopping. Along with that, most fashion stores across Vietnam have an included online selling platform attached to their local business as it is so beneficial to their sales, some shops even skip the traditional way of opening a local shop and go online as their only selling platform. E-commerce shows its potential in becoming the leading option for shopping – dynamic, flexible, and easy to use. E-commerce is something that each member of our group uses daily and is so intertwined with the development of the technological field, this is why we’ve decided to invest time and effort in this project.

Motivation

Since E-commerce is becoming more and more popular and also really common to us, we are interested in how it works and the principle behind it and since we use it almost every day, we want to see E-commerce not only as the user but also as a developer of one – managing and developing such platform. In order for E-commerce to be a success, lots of things need to be done. It needs to be appealing and interesting to the buyers which requires understanding and keeping up with the current and upcoming trends and thoroughly studying the market as well as creating a brand signature and advertising to make the brand well-known. E-commerce is also changing some of the ways we approach some of current IT trends, such as digitizing wallet (E-wallet) – offering more payment options and a faster way of purchasing; or AI chatbot (simulated human-like conversations) – a 24/7, user-friendly AI that can provide immediate responses which dramatically reduce wait times and make product recommendations based on a user’s browsing history. This would show any employers that the developer understands the client and what can be done in order to satisfy their needs, as well as knowing what is the best approach for the company with the minimum risks possible.

Examples of similar systems are big online shopping platforms such as Amazon, Shopee, etc.… some systems have also introduced the concept of peer-to-peer selling like eBay, Chotot,… E-commerce is a highly competitive industry, so extreme that a 3-second difference in loading a page can determine whether the customer thinks this is a good site for shopping or not. A good E-commerce site needs to be done well both in website performance and product variety. An online shop can be offering a wide range of products with the highest quality but poor performance websites still make them a “bad” business and vice versa. Our project doesn’t have much differ from other existed E-commerce site out there, out site focuses on selling authentic sportswear and casual products coming from large brand like Nike, Adidas,… with a reasonable price. Aims

We aim to explore the ways in which E-Commerce can be used to provide an effective platform for businesses and customers alike. We will be looking at the various aspects that make up this type of platform, such as the user interface, payment methods, customer service, and marketing strategies. We will also be looking into the various technologies available to aid in developing an E-Commerce platform, such as digital wallets and AI chatbots. Because there are so many factors to make a successful platform, we have quite a few goals to achieve in this project.

Goals

Research and analyze existing E-Commerce platforms.

Learning from past and present successful generations is never a bad idea. As a first step in achieving our goal, we will research and analyze existing E-Commerce platforms in Vietnam, such as Shopee and Lazada. We will be looking at their user interface design, payment methods, customer service, and other features that make them successful. We will also be researching the various other technologies that they used to develop an effective E-Commerce platform.

 

Collect information from users and developers.

We will be conducting interviews with E-Commerce platform users in order to get feedback on their experience with these platforms. We will also be conducting interviews with E-Commerce platform developers in order to gain insights into the development process. Design and develop an E-Commerce platform. 

Using the information gathered from our research and interviews, we will design a platform that is effective and easy to use. Using the latest technologies available in this project is our best bet. Test and evaluate the platform. 

Once the platform has been developed, we will be testing and evaluating it in order to ensure that it meets the requirements of the users and developers. This will involve testing the user interface design, payment methods, customer service, and other features of the platform.

Risks

There are a lot of risks coming with developing an e-commerce website. Since its focus is selling products online, it is critical that we need to keep the site secured and protected in every way. The first risk can be a data breach, hackers can breach their way into the system, obtaining both employee and customer credentials and planting malware in the company's official emails. The second risk is human error, human error is the most common risk out of all risks, employee mistakes can cause data loss or accidentally publishing confidential information, which competitor companies can exploit. The third risk can be DDOS, since E-commerce is a very competitive industry, it’s no surprise that business is very vulnerable to DDOS, which causes runtime error and unable to access the platform, or worse, causes physical damage to the server. All of the risks above have one thing in common – once they happen, they will cause serious problems, costing lots of time and money to fix. Some ways we can mitigate those risks are backing up data in case of a breakdown, using applications that can restore and recover data quickly so that it minimizes downtime, using security platforms to prevent data breaches and DDOS, train staff, and having a protocol to deal with downtime,…
