E-Commerce

Project team

We will need a team of skilled professional developers to complete this project. An ideal project team should include a project manager/administrator, software developers, UI/UX designers, testers, researchers, and incident response/support staff.

 

  • Project management and administration: The team should also have a manager and administrative staff to handle the project's day-to-day operations. Overseeing the overall project, including scheduling, budgeting, and communicating with the team, are jobs they will take care of. Also, the supervisor should have experience in project management and be able to coordinate the different tasks of the team in a timely manner. In other words, they will be responsible for ensuring that all tasks are completed on time. The administrative staff should be able to handle the paperwork and other administrative functions of the project.   

 

  • Software Developers: liable for developing the E-Commerce platform, including the user interface, payment methods, customer service, and other features. The developer should be knowledgeable in a variety of programming languages and be capable of integrating various technologies into the platform.  

 

  • UI/UX Designers: responsible for designing the platform's user interface and experience. It is essential that they understand user experience design principles and be able to develop a visually appealing and easy-to-use interface.  

 

  • Testers: accountable for testing the platform in order to ensure that it meets the requirements of the users and developers. They should be experienced in software testing and capable of identifying potential bugs.  

 

  • Researchers: the researchers, such as existing E-Commerce platforms and technologies, will research various aspects of the project. Their industry knowledge and ability to provide insights and feedback are essential.  

 

  • Incident response and support: finally, the team should have an incident response and support team in order to handle any issues that may arise during the development and deployment of the platform. They should be able to respond quickly to any incidents and support the platform's users.
